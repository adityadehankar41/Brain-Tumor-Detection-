Advanced Brain Tumor Treatment Technologies

Innovative approaches for better patient outcomes

Department of Neurosurgery is at the forefront of implementing cutting-edge technologies for brain tumor treatment. These innovative approaches aim to improve patient outcomes through less invasive procedures and more precise interventions.

Key Treatment Technologies

Laser Interstitial Thermal Therapy (LITT)

A minimally invasive procedure that uses laser energy delivered through a small incision to heat and destroy tumor tissue. Our Clinic was an early adopter of this technology, which can be used for hard-to-reach tumors.

Intraoperative MRI

Allows surgeons to capture real-time MRI images during surgery, enabling more complete tumor removal while preserving healthy tissue. This technology helps confirm the extent of tumor removal before completing the procedure.

Advanced Imaging Techniques

We utilizes several advanced imaging technologies including:

  • Functional MRI (fMRI): Maps brain activity to help surgeons avoid critical areas
  • Diffusion tensor imaging: Visualizes white matter tracts to guide surgical approaches
  • Magnetic resonance spectroscopy: Analyzes biochemical changes in tumors

Awake Brain Surgery

Performed while the patient is conscious but sedated, allowing surgeons to monitor brain function in real-time during tumor removal. This approach helps preserve critical functions like speech and motor skills.

Robotic-Assisted Surgery

Enhances surgical precision through robotic systems that assist neurosurgeons in performing complex procedures with greater accuracy.

Benefits of These Technologies

  • Reduced invasiveness compared to traditional open surgery
  • Shorter hospital stays and recovery times
  • Improved accuracy in tumor targeting
  • Better preservation of healthy brain tissue
  • Reduced risk of complications
  • Improved quality of life outcomes for patients
