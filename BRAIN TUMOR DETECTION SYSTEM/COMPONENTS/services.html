Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of services for the diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing care of patients with brain tumors.

Advanced Diagnostics

State-of-the-art imaging technologies including MRI, CT, PET scans, and specialized neuroimaging to accurately diagnose and characterize brain tumors.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Cutting-edge surgical techniques including Laser Interstitial Thermal Therapy (LITT), awake craniotomies, and endoscopic approaches performed by our expert neurosurgeons.

Precision Radiation Therapy

Advanced radiation treatments including Stereotactic Radiosurgery (SRS), Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT), and proton therapy to target tumors while sparing healthy tissue.

Medical Oncology

Personalized chemotherapy protocols, targeted therapies, immunotherapies, and access to clinical trials for emerging treatments.

Comprehensive Support

Rehabilitation services, psychological support, nutritional counseling, and social services to address all aspects of care during and after treatment.

Long-term Follow-up

Ongoing monitoring and survivorship services to manage recovery, address late effects of treatment, and provide continuous support.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of brain tumors do you treat?

We treat all types of brain tumors, both benign and malignant, including but not limited to gliomas, meningiomas, acoustic neuromas, pituitary tumors, metastatic brain tumors, and many others.

How soon can I get an appointment?

We understand the urgency of brain tumor diagnosis and strive to see new patients within 48-72 hours of referral. For urgent cases, we can often accommodate same-day consultations.

Will my insurance cover my treatment?

We work with most major insurance providers. Our financial counselors will work with you to understand your coverage and explore all available options to ensure you receive the care you need.

What is the recovery time after brain tumor surgery?

Recovery times vary depending on the type of surgery, tumor location, and individual factors. Many patients undergoing minimally invasive procedures may return home within 1-3 days, while more complex surgeries might require a longer hospital stay of 5-7 days. Our team will provide you with specific information based on your treatment plan.

Do you offer clinical trials?

Yes, we actively participate in numerous clinical trials testing innovative approaches to brain tumor treatment. Our team will discuss whether you might be eligible for any current trials as part of your treatment planning.
